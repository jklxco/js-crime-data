Explore Your Neighborhood's Safety

Welcome to Crime Data, where you can gain valuable insights into the safety of your neighborhood. Our innovative platform combines the power of Google Maps with crime data from the UK Police API, allowing you to visualize and understand the safety landscape within a 1-mile radius of your chosen location.

Key Features:

Interactive Crime Mapping:

Explore an interactive map that provides a visual representation of reported crimes in your vicinity. Easily navigate through different crime categories, time periods, and locations to get a comprehensive overview.

User-Friendly Search:

Enter your address or simply click on the map to set your location. Our website will instantly generate a detailed crime map, helping you make informed decisions about the safety of your surroundings.

Detailed Crime Information:

Get detailed information about each reported crime, including the type of crime, date, time, and location. Our platform aims to empower you with accurate and up-to-date information for a clearer understanding of your neighborhood's safety.

Customizable Filters:

Tailor your crime map to your preferences. Filter crime data based on specific categories, such as burglary, anti-social behavior, or violent crimes. You can also narrow down results by date range for a more focused analysis.

Community Insights:

Join our community discussions to share experiences, report concerns, and engage with neighbors. Together, we can work towards creating safer and more secure neighborhoods.
